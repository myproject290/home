Question 5
Which animals come to your home or your neighborhood?
Answer:
Animals like dogs, cats, cows, etc., often come to my home.

Question 6
Have you ever shared food with the animals who come to your home?
Answer:
Yes, I daily feed a cow with roti. I also provide grains to the birds.

Question 7
Why do you think we should not hurt or tease animals?
Answer:
We should never hurt or tease any animal because like us they also feel bad on teasing or hurting.

Question 8
What have you learned from your family and from whom?
Answer:
Answer: I learned good manners from my grandmother, discipline and yoga from my grandfather, sharing and being friendly from my father and mother.

Question 9
Has anyone learned anything from you?
Answer: Yes, I have taught my grandmother to operate a mobile.

Question 10
How do you help your family?
Answer:
I help my mother in making my bed, washing fruits and vegetables. I help my grandparents by giving them their spectacles, medicines, and other things that they need.

Question 11
How do family members show their love and care for each other?
Answer: Family members show love by caring and helping each other. They spend time together and share affection.

Question 12
What games do you play with your friends?
Answer:
Hint: I like to play different games like bat-ball, football, hide and seek, etc., with my friends.

Question 13
What do you learn from elders in your family?
Answer:
I learn the following things from elders in my family:

1. Good behaviour
2. Moral values
3. Traditions
4. Greet guests
5. Respecting elders
6. Skills such as singing, dancing, playing, etc.

Question 14
We care for many others who are not in our family. Who are they? How do we care for them?
Answer:
Besides our family members, we also care for our friends and neighbours. We help them when they are in need.
